[Transactional Emails]: 
    -system-triggered emails, 
    -sent automatically to an individual based on a specific action or event they've initiated.
    -contain important information for the recipient.
    -e.g. password reset emails, account activation emails, notifications, ...


[High-Volume Emails]: 
    -large quantity of emails sent in bulk, often within a short period of time.
    -not individually triggered, but sending emails in bulks (hundreds or thousands).
    -are common for large businesses, e-commerce platforms, and marketing campaigns,
    -Requires Scalability: needs to send bulk of emails without delays or failures.
    -Deliverability Concerns: needs to send bulk of emails without being spams.
